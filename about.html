COLLEGE LIFE

Hope you enjoyed playing our game!

The Game of College Life is a simulation to help college students' financial literacy. Through a Monopoly-based game, students develop a deeper understanding and empathy of students from different backgrounds.

The game starts with the players choosing a persona as a first-generation or a legacy college student. With the roll of a dice and scenario cards, the players move through the board, experiencing the broad categories of academics, college living expenses, and personal finances.

This website delves further into the concepts introduced by the game by personality types. The Game of College Life not only educates students about a healthy balance but also sparks conversations about economic differences and opportunities.
